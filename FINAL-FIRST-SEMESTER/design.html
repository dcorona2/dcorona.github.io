The Fourth Stage of the Web Development Cycle

Welcome to the fourth stage of the Web Development Cycle: Design!

In this stage of the Web Development Cycle, the designers will be planning on how they want to communicate with the sites audience. This can consist of things such as headlines which could attract a readers attention. The overall goal of this stage is to make things as appealing to the reader as possible by making things easy to understand.
