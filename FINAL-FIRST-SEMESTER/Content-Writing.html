The Third Stage of the Web Development Cycle

Welcome to the third stage of the Web Development Cycle: Content Writing!

Now that the planning stage is done, we head to the content writing section. During this part of the cycle, the developers and designers will begin to determine the websites appearance and style. This can also include them planning on how the site will be laid out.

Below are the many things that the developers may think about during this stage:

  • Site's color scheme
  • Font style
  • Graphics
  • Buttons
  • And many more
