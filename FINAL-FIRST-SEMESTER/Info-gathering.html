The First Stage of the Web Development Cycle

Welcome to the first stage of the Web Development Cycle: Info Gathering!

During this stage of the Web Development Cycle, both the developers and the person behind the site will be discussing the sites purpose. To do this, they will gather information about many things including the sites audience, what goals it will accomplish (brand recognition, sales, or user engagement), and what kind of content will be featured on the site. This is done to make sure that both the developers and designers are on the same page when designing the website.
